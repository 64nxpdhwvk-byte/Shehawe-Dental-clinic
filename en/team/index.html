Dental Team

Clear roles and expertise

Dr. Ahmed Khairy

Founder of Shehawy Dental Clinic and a 6th of October University graduate (Class of 2008). He delivers precise treatment with clear steps and easy-to-understand explanations, prioritising patient comfort and long-term results.

Dr. Ahmed continually develops his skills through specialized endodontic courses and believes that accurate diagnosis and thoughtful planning are the foundation of any successful treatment.

Founder

Dr. Hesham El-Nouby

Orthodontics specialist and 6th of October University graduate (Class of 2009). He provides detailed orthodontic plans with regular follow-ups to achieve a balanced smile and healthy bite, while keeping patients comfortable and informed.

Holder of a Laser MSc from Cairo University, Dr. Hesham blends practical experience with modern techniques to deliver safe, stable, and long-lasting orthodontic results.

Dr. Hend Gamal El-Din

General dentist and Alexandria University graduate (Class of 2012). She creates a calm, friendly experience for children, helping them overcome fear and enjoy visiting the dentist.

Dr. Hend has advanced her specialty through pediatric dentistry courses and heads the dental department at Abis 10th unit with the Ministry of Health.
